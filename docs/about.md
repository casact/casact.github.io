# Joing Us

The Open-Source Projects Working Group supports the Casualty Actuarial
Society's open-source initiatives, helping members build, maintain, and
share actuarial tools and research code.

## Design Philosophy

The Casualty Actuarial Society has accumulated over a century's worth of knowledge, available through its extensive [research library](https://www.casact.org/research). However, much of this information has never been made available in the form of open source packages. By transforming theory into code, the Open-Source Projects Working Group aims to build practical solutions for the modern era.

While the [chainladder package](https://chainladder-python.readthedocs.io/) has received much of our attention, our goal is to design libraries for other areas of practice. These would include, but are not limited to:

- Pricing
- Workers' compensation
- Reinsurance
- Capital modeling

Many of these packages will be the first open-source libraries released in their respective practice areas, and by volunteering with the working group, you have the opportunity to make a positive impact on the actuarial profession.
